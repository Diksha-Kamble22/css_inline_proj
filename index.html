You may follow the below-mentioned ten rules and regulations while driving to reduce the risk of accidents and ensure safe driving:
  • Always wear a seatbelt The most important rule is to always strap on a seatbelt when you hit the wheel. Section 138 (3) CMVR 177 MVA of the Motor Vehicles Act lays down the specific fine for not fastening a seatbelt.
  • Avoid distractions While you are behind the wheel, ensure that your main focus is on driving. Do not get distracted by loud music or by talking on the phone while driving.
  • Do not cross the speed limits The goal of setting a speed limit is to ensure the safety of passengers in the vehicle as well as pedestrians. Besides, driving at high speed, even on roads with less traffic, may result in the driver losing control or a sudden brake fail, thereby causing an accident.
